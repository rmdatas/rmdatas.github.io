BluStream

 

R.M Data Solutions offer BluStream installations for your home audiovisual system. Blustream is a device used to a single HDMI feed across multiple television screens.

This service is perfect for splitting a Foxtel box*, or an amp to multiple televisions around the house. This means you can have Foxtel, Apple TV, or your speaker amplifier split between as many rooms you want, and use the one remote for each device for whichever room you are in. This method has been proved to be much more cost-effective option opposed to buying a second Foxtel box or streaming device.

 

*Steaming though BluStream does not provide the customer with multiple devices (e.g. Foxtel boxes) that can be used separately, it allows for one device to be viewed and operated from multiple screens.
