Aerial Installations

 

Our fully-licensed technicians will carefully install an antenna on your residence or business, in the most efficient, and least intrusive location possible. You will have the choice of up 2-16 antenna points throughout your property, with a splitter installed in an accessible location within the property.

Using the best quality wall plates and cabling, we ensure that you will be receiving the highest efficacy and clarity out of your new installation. We will also tune and configure your current televisions, at no extra cost, to allow for a seamless installation.
