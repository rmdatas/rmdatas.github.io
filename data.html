Relocation of Existing/Extra Data Points

 

Wi-Fi is an incredible resource in the modern home, but it is not without its complications. Slow speeds, multiple users, and rooms out of range. These are all factors that can be avoided or reduced with the installation of extra Network/Ethernet points. These points are connected to your modem using high quality, high-speed cable, which allows for a direct connection to reduce interference. Offering optimum speeds from your network provider.

R.M Data Solutions can install extra Network/Ethernet point in any location of your residences or business, using the best quality equipment and cabling at an extremely competitive price.

We pride ourselves on our ability to get cables into any location without damaging the existing property or having visible cabling.

Adding a new Network/Ethernet point could be easier, cheaper and less intrusive than you may have first thought.

We will also test and configure the data point to your chosen device to ensure a seamless transition from wireless to hard wired.
