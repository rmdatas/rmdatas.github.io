Extra Data Points/Data Point Relocations

 

Wi-Fi is an incredible resource in the modern home, but it is not without its complications. Slow speeds, multiple users, and rooms out of range, are all factors that can be avoided with the installation of extra Network/Ethernet points. These points are connected to your modem using high quality, high speed cable, which allows for a direct connection and a lack of interference offering optimum speeds from your network provider. At R.M Data Solutions we will install extra data points in any location of your residence or business, using the best quality equipment and cabling, we pride ourselves on our ability to get cables into any location without damaging the existing property or having visible cabling. Adding a new Network/Ethernet point could be easier and less intrusive than you may assume.   We will also test and configure the data point to your chosen device to ensure a seamless transition from wireless to hard wired.
