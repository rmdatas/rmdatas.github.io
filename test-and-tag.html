At R.M Data Solutions our goal is to provide the highest quality test and tagging, at the most cost effective prices. Though using a wide range of suppliers we have been able to reduce our overhead costs which in-turn we can pass onto the customer.

 

Testing and Tagging is the process of inspecting and testing all in service electrical equipment for personal safety.

 

Occupational health and safety legislations states that some businesses, contractors and sole traders operating in a working environment must have a regular test and tag performed on their electrical equipment if they want to be compliant with the AS/NZS 3760:2010. This is to ensure the safety of your equipment and more importantly the safety of your employees, yourself and anyone else who may come in contact with your equipment.
