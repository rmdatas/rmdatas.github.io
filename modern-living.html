BluStream

 

R.M Data Solutions offer BluStream installations for your home audiovisual system. Blustream is a device used to a single HDMI feed across multiple television screens.

This service is perfect for splitting a Foxtel box*, or an amp to multiple televisions around the house. This means you can have Foxtel, Apple TV, or your speaker amplifier split between as many rooms you want, and use the one remote for each device for whichever room you are in. This method has been proved to be much more cost-effective option opposed to buying a second Foxtel box or streaming device.

 

*Steaming though BluStream does not provide the customer with multiple devices (e.g. Foxtel boxes) that can be used separately, it allows for one device to be viewed and operated from multiple screens.

 

Access Control/Keyless Entry

 

As technology changes so does the modern home and business.

Previously the use of a keycard to unlock an entry was a luxury only seen in high range hotels, but now with new technology, like domestic and commercial access systems, keyless entry can be offered to any property.

R.M Data Solution can improve the functionality and the efficiency of your home, allowing you to enter your home or business with the almost effortless swipe of a card.

Single Console, playable on multiple screens

 

The ability to walk around the house with a single controller and be able to play your console on any televisions within your property, without the time-consuming hassle of moving and reconnecting the console from where it is originally stationed.

Has been a dream for console players for a long time. That dream is now a reality.

By using new technology, R.M Data Solutions are able to split your console feed to any television in your home and transmit your controller signal back to the device.

Letting you play in any location, hassle-free.
